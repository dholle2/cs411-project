AirBNB and Safety in Chicago

Our project will allow users to search for AirBNBs in the Chicagoland area and see their relative safety rating. The safety rating is calculated by looking at recent crime incidents in Chicago that have occurred recently. Users can also find cafes near their AirBNB.
